About Us

A company of first choice. For all seedlings and all kinds of vegtables.

Carol's Seedlings & Vegtables is an organisation which was established in 2018 by our core Founder Vimbai Kaitano. The organisation strives to improve the agriculture industry by producing high quality seedlings for all different stakeholders who posses interests in farming. We provide a wide range of seedlings and they are very affordable. Recently the organisation ventured into educational programmes with the aim of educating its clients who are new in the farming industry. In addition, as for for anyone who does not have any skill or knowledge in farming but has interests in farming, well, you have come to the right place.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide our customers great service by providing them with healthy and high quality seedlings. The company also aims at educating all the farmers that we supply with seedlings on how to keep their plants healthy and in great shape.
